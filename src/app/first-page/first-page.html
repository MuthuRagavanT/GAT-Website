Dr. Kumaraswamy S

Professor & Head, Department of Computer Science Engineering

Professional Experience

Dr. Kumaraswamy S. is the Head of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Global Academy of Technology. He brings extensive expertise in cloud computing, specializing in distributed cloud centers and optimization algorithms for virtual machine placement. His career is marked by significant contributions to wireless sensor networks, cybersecurity, and deep learning. Under his leadership, the department has fostered innovation and student collaboration, particularly in cloud computing, machine learning, and artificial intelligence.
